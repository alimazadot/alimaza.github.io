hello, my name is Ali!


I’m an artist and writer who creates comics! I’m the author of the webcomic Duck and Kitten, a comedy about good and evil, and am now seeking to publish my first middle grade graphic novel. In 2023, I was named one of the top 3 Venezuelan cartoonists by Lygno Productions. That year, my work was featured in The 99, a comics anthology led by Eisner-nominated editor David Danemen. In 2024, I was named a LANDO finalist by the de Groot Foundation for a $7000 grant for writers exploring the topics of migration, immigration and refugees.

fun facts about me:


🌍 I’m an immigrant from Anzoátegui, Venezuela and currently based in Barcelona, Spain.

💛 My name is pronounced like “Muhammad Ali”.

🌈 I identify as genderfluid and prefer people to alternate between they, she and he pronouns when referring to me. Don’t worry if this is too hard for you, though - it’s impossible to misgender me! :)
